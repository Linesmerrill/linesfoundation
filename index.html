About Us

A college degree is becoming more of a requirement in today's society, but the cost of education continues to rise. Our goal is to provide financial assistance so that money is not an excuse to skip out on an education.

The Lines Foundation was founded on the principal that college is expensive and over two-thirds of full-time students need some sort of financial aid. We believe that some students fall between the cracks for federal aid. This organization was developed to confront this issue and not let the student suffer.

We believe in inspiring students

The Lines Foundation was developed to identify those in need and give them the opportunity at a college education. This does not mean that because your parents make too much money you will not be selected. Our selection board takes into account Need, Academics, and Extra Curricular Activities before making a selection.
